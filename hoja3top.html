3. Lake uru uru

near Oruro a big lake with a Rich biodiversity can be found. The uru uru lake is one of the most wonderful bodies of water in Bolivia. You can without problem see his glorious fauna and flora, in summer you can see flamingos!! The titicaca lake is connected by the river desaguadero to the uru uru lake. The lake is perfect for boating and fishing. It contains a big and amazing large number of fish. It is situated at an elevation of 3,686 m, its surface area is 214 km. In conclusion Characterized by its height, more than 3,600 m.a.s.l., and by its 214 km², it is a huge ecosystem of great tourist attraction and with a large number of fish species.
