4. La Paz

If you are going on a trip to Bolivia you obviously should know about La Paz. La Paz is the beautiful capital of Bolivia. In La Paz the indigenous culture is very strong and alive, The native Aymara form the majority in La Paz, whose colorful traditional outfits can be seen proudly on display. La Paz is like a connector to travel to the other touristy parts of Bolivia. The nightlife in La Paz is awesome too. For his series of boisterous backpacker orientated party hostels. If you also love food La Paz is your place, his foodie is surprisingly robust perfect for an exquisite high altitude dining experience In La Paz they also have fairs like the Feria de Alasitas, which takes place every year.Bolivians from all over the country buy miniature items to offer to Ekeko, the Aymaran God of abundance, hoping he will bring good fortune and wealth into their lives.
