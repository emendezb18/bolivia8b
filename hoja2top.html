2. Sajama national park

if you want to visit an outstanding park with a lot of alpacas, towering volcanoes, and bizarre high altitude flora. And if you are an adventurer who likes to go to amazing places people often don’t go You should go to the sajama national park. There are a bunch of wonderful lakes like: Huanakota Lake, quirky Queñoa forests, gushing geysers and if you are searching for extreme challenges, you should try to go to the different series of volcanoes. The national park is home to Bolivia’s highest peak, the ominous 6,542 meter Nevado Sajama which looms over the landscape at every turn. The best time to go to the sajama national park is between April or November where there is little or no rain. If you go between January and March it’s almost impossible to visit because of the heavy rain.
